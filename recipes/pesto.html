Pesto

Fresh herbs make any meal instantly more flavorful and beautiful. Let's make pesto from them!It's nice for grilled vegetables, pasta, spaghetti squash, salads, eggs, toasted bread, pizza, sandwiches… you get the idea. With just a few easy steps, it makes anything an instant tasty meal, whether you feel like cooking or not.

Pesto Ingredients

  • Fresh basil
  • Garlic
  • Pine nuts
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese

How to Make Pesto From Scratch

  1. Pulse the nuts, lemon juice, and garlic in the food processor until they’re finely chopped.
  2. Add the basil and pulse again.
  3. Next, drizzle in the olive oil with the food processor blade still running.
  4. Finally, add grated Parmesan cheese and process briefly until combined.
